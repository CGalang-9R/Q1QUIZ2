☀︎ Tangled ☀︎



Image from: Fandom

An enchanting adventure of Rapunzel, a lost princess who was kidnapped at birth for her long, blonde, and magical hair. Mother Gothel forced her to live and stay in her tower to keep her under her control. As her 18th birthday comes along, she longs more than ever to venture to the outside world, though Mother Gothel disapproves of this. She eventually escapes from her tower with the help of Flynn Rider, a wanted thief, to celebrate her 18th birthday. Will Mother Gothel find out? Will she have to face the challenges of the real world? Will she discover the truth about herself?
